APFC (Automatic Power Factor Control) Panel

Automatic Power Factor Control Panels are mainly used for the improvement of Power Factors. Power Factor can be explained as ratio of active power to apparent power and it is a key factor in measuring electrical consumption. Everyone knows that how costly electricity has become in present time. Therefore it becomes utmost important to cut down on electrical consumption for reducing expenditure. APFC Panels are effectively and automatically manage quickly changing and scattered loads along with the retention of high Power Factor.

WE MANUFACTURE , SUPPLY OF THE APFC PANELS UP-TO 1000KVA RANGE.

  • Microprocessor based automatic controller with power factor display.
  • In-built time delay for switching ON of a capacitor bank to avoid impulse signal operation. All capacitor feeders have Auto / Manual mode facility with fuse protection and isolation.
  • Improvement of power factor up to optimum levels is guaranteed.
  • Prevents leading Power Factor in low load conditions.
  • Provision of detuned reactors the panels for reliable operation in presence of harmonics.
